All About Onyx

Onyx, with its deep black color, symbolizes strength, protection, and resilience. It has long been used as a protective stone to ward off negative energy and promote emotional stability.

In its raw form, Onyx retains its powerful, dark tones and is often associated with grounding energy. Many believe raw Onyx provides strength during challenging times and encourages self-control.

Onyx’s sleek black color adds a bold and sophisticated look to any jewelry piece. It’s often used in rings, necklaces, and cufflinks for a classic, timeless appeal that exudes elegance and strength.
