All About Peridot

Peridot is known for its bright green hue, symbolizing renewal, growth, and vitality. Its vibrant color is associated with positive energy and abundance, making it a symbol of good fortune.

In its raw form, Peridot radiates a fresh and lively green, believed to enhance personal strength and dispel negativity. Many use raw Peridot to attract prosperity and foster emotional healing.
