All About Tanzanite

Tanzanite is famous for its rich blue-violet color, symbolizing transformation, insight, and higher consciousness. It is one of the rarest gemstones, found only in Tanzania, making it highly sought after for its exclusivity.

In its raw state, Tanzanite's striking blue-violet tones are believed to enhance spiritual awareness and intuition. Many use raw Tanzanite for meditation and to connect with higher realms of consciousness.
