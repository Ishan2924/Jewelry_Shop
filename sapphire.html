All About Sapphire

Sapphire is renowned for its deep, royal blue color, symbolizing wisdom, loyalty, and nobility. It has long been favored by royalty and is associated with truth and clarity of thought.

Raw Sapphire, with its rich, blue tones, is said to bring spiritual insight and mental focus. Many believe that raw Sapphire helps to enhance communication and understanding, making it a stone of wisdom and truth.
