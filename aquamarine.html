All About Aquamarine

Aquamarine is admired for its light blue, oceanic color, symbolizing clarity, calm, and serenity. Its soothing blue hues evoke the tranquility of the sea, making it a popular gemstone for promoting relaxation and peace.

In its raw state, Aquamarine's pale, translucent blue tones are said to enhance clear communication and encourage courage. It is often used to promote mental clarity and emotional balance.
