All About Turquoise

Turquoise is treasured for its beautiful blue-green hue, symbolizing protection, wisdom, and strength. It has been used as a talisman for centuries, believed to bring good fortune and guard against negative energy.

In its raw form, Turquoise retains a rugged, earthy charm, often used in healing practices for its calming and protective properties. It is said to bring emotional balance and protect against negativity.
